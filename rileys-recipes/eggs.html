Muffin Recipe

Sad eggs recipe! Kill the baby chicken, then fry, boil, scramble, or cook to your liking!

Ingredients:

  • 5 eggs
  • 1 Large knife
  • Garlic Salt

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water or bring a pan to medium heat
  2. Crack open your eggs, feed the shell to your dog
  3. Spray pan with baking spray
  4. Put egg inthe boiling water or pan
  5. Cook for 5-10 minutes
