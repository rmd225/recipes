Muffin Recipe

Yummy muffin recipe! Comes with blueberries and pants, healthy and easy to bake!

Ingredients:

  • Small pants
  • 1/2 pint blueberries
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Mix together the eggs, flour, sugar, and blueberries in a meduim sized bowl
  3. Spray muffin tin with baking spray
  4. Pour mixture into pan, bake for 25 min
  5. Put pants on the muffin, she finna be thicc
